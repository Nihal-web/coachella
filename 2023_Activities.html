Carpoolchella participants travel from all corners of the Earth to promote the idea that riding with friends is the only way to travel! Looking to unwind and recharge before attending the festival? To help you connect with yourself and others, our wellness programming includes daily yoga, meditation, and sound healing sessions. Sessions are held on Thursday from 5pm to 8pm and on Friday - Sunday from 7am to 3pm. Don't forget to bring a mat!
