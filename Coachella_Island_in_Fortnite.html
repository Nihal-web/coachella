Coachella Island in Fortnite

Introducing Coachella Island in Fortnite, where the weekend never ends. Explore an evolving oasis filled with music, art, and games inspired by the festival. Coachella Island opens April 14 at 12pm PT. 

Coachella Reimagined

  • Immerse in a vibrant, music-reactive environment featuring the iconic desert mountains, Polo Fields, palm trees and golden hour sunsets
  • Groove to a special soundtrack featuring music by 2023 artist Porter Robinson
  • Explore the Art Park and iconic festival installations like Spectra, ferris wheel, the balloon chain, and more
  • Discover new festival installations by Maggie West and Güvenç Özel, reimagined for Coachella Island
  • Challenge yourself with the Mirage Time Warp minigame as you speedrun past iconic Coachella moments throughout time
  • Grab your friends and play the team-vs-team dance mini-game
  • Enjoy new art installations by Fortnite creators, Alliance
  • Get featured in the Fortography gallery. ​​Share your screenshots by April 18 with the #Fortography hashtag on Twitter and/or the “Fortography” post flair on r/FortNiteBR.
  • Shop exclusive 2023 Coachella-inspired Outfits and in-game items at the Merch Tent
  • Level up with special Coachella Island-inspired quests that earn you in-game assets

The Weekend Never Ends

Be a Coachella Island Creator

Coachella Island is home to the best virtual experiences crafted by independent creators. Starting now, Fortnite Creators can submit their Creative map for a chance to be featured on Coachella Island. Stretch your imagination and create a world inspired by the magic of the festival and the power of music. Every two weeks, we will feature a new map on the Island and promote it from Coachella’s official channels.
