Advance Sale Payment Plan Info

1. Payment Plans are available for all orders over $100 while supplies last!

2. Only $5 fee per installment for payment plan. No added interest!

3. You may pay in full if you do not want to use the payment plan.

4. You must select the payment plan during the checkout process. If you miss this, you'll be charged in full. There will be no account adjustments.
 
5. Exact payment amounts may vary by a few cents due to rounding, but the total due will not exceed order total.

6. Automated payments may take up to 72 hours to process.

7. No refunds, cancellations or exchanges.

8. Your order will not be eligible for the Official Waitlist until it has been paid in full.
 
9. For any order that defaults on their payment plan, all money will be issued as a Coachella Credit via FanAccount. Please note, the funds        will expire one year from time of issuance. 

Missed Payments

1. If your payment is declined, you will have a 10-day grace period to pay.

2. If payment is not received by the 10th day after the initial charge is run, your order may be canceled at any time.

3. Defaulting on payments will result in cancellation of your order and all money paid issued as a Coachella Credit that can be used towards future              Coachella purchases. No exceptions. It is your responsibility to manage your payment plan and to check your credit card and bank statements for            charges. 

Getting Your Order

  • Delivery dates and other details will be announced after the 2024 line up is announced and the 2024 website is launched. Stay tuned!
  • Orders will not be re-shipped under any circumstances.
  • Orders unable to be delivered under any circumstances (lost/stolen/errors/forgot/dog scared the postman away/wrong address, etc.) must be picked up at Will Call.
  • Orders at Will Call must be picked up by the original purchaser (the name on the account). Please keep that in mind when ordering. No name changes allowed. No Alternative box office pick ups allowed. No exceptions.
  • Delivery fees will not be refunded for undeliverable orders or for orders that are delivered/set to will call.
  • Shipments “Returned to Sender” may choose a one-time reshipment offer for a fee of $29 up until our shipment cut off. Shipment must have been received back by sender before request can be considered. Exceptions may apply. Absolutely no name changes allowed. No guarantee of reshipment.
  • Orders assigned/changed/ending up at Will Call must be picked up at the off-site Will Call/Box Office. Location/hours details to be posted closer to festival dates.
  • Government-issued photo ID and order confirmation are required for Will Call pick up.
  • $29 fee for Domestic & International shipping/delivery added to each order.
  • No shipping fee on orders containing just a Line Up Poster.
  • International customers will have the option to choose Will Call at time of purchase.
  • We recommend international travelers choose the Will Call option if you intend to travel prior to 4/1 (Weekend 1) or 4/8 (Weekend 2) to better ensure you don’t miss delivery.
  • Orders placed separately are shipped separately.
  • Orders are shipped in one [1] package per order (not per wristband). Condition of packaging is not guaranteed. The only guaranteed contents are what you ordered.
  • Wristband orders may be shipped in one complimentary commemorative box per order (not per pass or wristband within an order). The condition of the box is not guaranteed.
  • Commemorative box and/or packaging are not considered part of the order.
  • For Hotel Package guests, wristbands and shuttle passes will not be mailed in advance. All materials will be available for pickup at the hotel during designated Travel Package Check In hours, which will be provided prior to arrival.

All advance sale purchase codes will be distributed beginning at 8am PT, on Friday, June 16th, 2023. If you can’t find your code, visit prereg.coachella.com to resend or sign up. For further presale ticketing support, email coachella@tunespeak.email

We recommend using an actual credit card, not a debit card. If you are using a debit card, make sure you know your daily withdrawal limit. If you exceed that amount, your card may be rejected and order lost. Contact your bank to inquire about your debit card limit.
