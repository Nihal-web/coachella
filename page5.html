2023 Camping

Camp onsite for non-stop fun. Build your own oasis or book one of four ready-to-go options.

Car Camping & Preferred Car Camping

What to expect
       Be sure to explore all the extra amenities and activities camping has to offer before and after the show each                                  night. Make friends for life from all over the world.

  • All car camping sites are 30'x10'
  • Unlimited friends/tents per site, if they fit!
  • One vehicle per site allowed.
  • One vehicle per site required.
  • Sites assigned in order of arrival. (review camping info & rules below for “wait together camp together” details!)
  • Preferred Car Camping sites located in lot 8. Don’t rush! Arrive at your leisure.
  • No RV/Motorhome camping. Passenger cars, trucks or vans only.
    Our most popular overnight option lets you park a vehicle on your campsite and build an awesome weekend oasis for you and your crew.

    Level up with a reserved “Preferred” site located in lot 8.

Tent camping

What to expect
        Make new friends from all over the country or the world!

  • Make new friends from all over the country or the world!
  • All tent camping sites are 15'x10'
  • Unlimited friends/tents per site, if they fit!
  • Sites assigned in order of arrival, separate from car camping.
  • Includes one (1) overnight parking space in an adjacent lot.
  • Add’l. vehicles must purchase Camping Companion Parking.
  • Short walk to visit your friends in Car Camping!
  • Review all Camping info & rules below!
  • Pitch your desert palace on a field of grass. Perfect for international travelers, campers without cars or folks who want a vehicle-free environment.

Lake Eldorado

2-Person Lodgeh
Ready to go 8’ x 12’ x 8’ wood lodge. Comes with two cots, sleeping bags and pillows, a lockable door, wood floor, 3 windows for added ventilation, indoor/outdoor lighting and storage cubbies. Includes 2 GA or 2 VIP passes.

4-Person Tentexp
Ready to go 12’x14′, fully pitched with four cots, sleeping bags and pillows, as well as chairs and a cooler. Includes 4 GA or 4 VIP passes.

  • 24-hr. concierge.
  • Includes one [1] overnight parking spot in an adjacent lot.

    Camp in style by booking a 4-person tent or 2-person lodge at our exclusive lakeside oasis.

Camping Rules & Info

Camping Rules & Info

Times/Checking In
1. Festival pass required for entry.
2. Camping is open Thu 9am-Mon 10am. Four nights stay!
3. Car/Tent check-in Thu 9am-2am; Fri & Sat 9am-8pm, Sun 9am-3pm.
4. Pedestrian check-in is open 24hrs.
5. Noise curfew starts at 1:30am.
6. Campers and vehicles are searched at check-in.
7. Everyone must be wearing a Festival Pass to enter camping.
8. No camping allowed between festival weekends.

Age limit

Campers must be 18+ over unless with a parent/legal guardian.

A legal guardian is a person who has the legal authority and corresponding duty to care for the personal and property interests of another person. An older sibling, older friend or friend of the family does not automatically count as a legal guardian. A legal guardian is someone who would be held legally responsible for you by a court of law.

Legal Guardianship in CA

Read More about Legal Guardianship

Vehicle Lockdown

1. No vehicle ins/outs.
2. No exit during lockdown hours Fri & Sat 9pm-2am and Sun 11pm-2am.
3. Vehicle lockdown hours may change depending on traffic conditions. Please be prepared to wait. If you must leave camping before 2am on Sunday night, you need to depart before 11pm

Arrive Together to Camp Together

1. Multiple cars wishing to camp in sites next to each other must arrive at the same time.
2.You should plan on meeting up prior to driving into camping at the same time.
3.This applies to tent-only campers too.
4. If you don’t check-in together, you won’t be able to camp next to each other. There are no saving spots.
5. It's essential your entire group drives into the toll booth/search area together.
6. As your cars proceed through search, you can continue into a limited-time waiting area.
7. Once your entire group arrives in the waiting area, you may proceed to the lanes to enter the camping lots.
8. Camping staff will try their best to accommodate directing your group to an appropriate location. Let them know how many cars (or tent only campers) are part of your group.

Misc./Good to Know

1. In order to accommodate all campers, you must camp where assigned. Be kind to other campers. Attempting to camp
2 anywhere else creates a significant disruption that negatively impacts everyone's comfort and safety.
3. All of your items must stay within the marked lines. Do not sleep in or allow any items to extend into the fire lane. Party!
4. Car Camping and Tent only camping is not mixed together.
5. Car Camping and Tent Camping areas are open to visitation by all Coachella attendees.
6. Lake Eldorado access is restricted to Lake Eldorado guests.
7. All campers are expected to behave in a manner that is courteous to other campers.
8. All times and information on this page are subject to change without notice.
9. Venue/promoter are not responsible for any materials misplaced, lost or stolen. It is recommended that you do not bring valuable items. If you do bring valuable items, please lock them in your car. Do not leave valuable items in your tent.


10. High winds and heavy dust are common in the Coachella Valley. Prepare your campsite by sealing, securing or collapsing     any structures or items that may be affected.

Food / Drinks


1. Compare may bring food & beverages.
2. No glass allowed/Review prohibited items below.
3. Campers 21+ may bring 1 case of beer cans or a box of wine.
4. If it appears that there is an excessive amount of alcohol for the number of people in the vehicle, or if anyone in the vehicle is under 21 years of age, we reserve the right to 5. deny entry of the vehicle or require you to surrender the alcohol.
6. Propane stoves allowed in Car Camping only. Review FAQ about cooking.
7. There is a General Store in camping with tons of stuff including microwaves and toasters for use!
8. Mobile Ice Sales will be available in all camp lots Thur 2pm – 5pm and Fri - Sun 9am – 3pm. Flag them down if you see them! Ice is also for sale in the General Store in Lot  9. Stay tuned for the Camping Eats & Drinks to be listed!
